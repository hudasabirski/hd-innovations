About HD Innovations

A brand built on meaning.

HD Innovations is a female-led photography and memory preservation company specializing in newborn and family portraits, curated memory kits, and modern, technology-enabled experiences.

Mission

Preserve life’s milestones with heirloom-grade craft—images to imprints—designed to last for generations.

Vision

Bring art and technology together at scale—redefining how memories are created, experienced, and passed down.

Meet the CEO

Huda Sabir

Huda Sabir leads HD Innovations with a passion for creativity, storytelling, and innovation—blending artistry with emotional value to turn moments into lasting legacies.

With a Bachelor’s degree in Photography (Hons) from the United Kingdom and international experience in building purpose-led brands, she shapes a future-forward approach to memory preservation.

Focus

Newborns • Families • Milestones

Approach

Crafted, digitalized, and curated

Customer segments

New parents, families, tourists, couples, and elderly loved ones.

Partners

Hospitals, malls, and family centers—plus suppliers and training programs.

What we deliver

Photography, prints, frames, 3D casts, and booth-based keepsakes.
